Professional Biography

Digital Content Strategist | Media Governance & Compliance Expert

Meher Ferchichi is a seasoned media professional and digital strategist with a career spanning over two decades of excellence in journalism, regulatory oversight, and digital transformation. Throughout his journey from field reporting to executive digital leadership, he has consistently demonstrated a unique ability to bridge the gap between traditional journalistic integrity and modern technological innovation.

2023 – Present

Executive Leadership & Digital Transformation

Currently serving as the Digital Content Strategy Manager & Media Trainer for Radio Tunisienne, Meher is the chief architect of the organization’s digital evolution. His leadership focuses on:

  • Infrastructure Modernization: Successfully leading the end-to-end project management, CMS (Content Management System) administration, and execution of Radio Tunisienne’s new suite of digital platforms.
  • Innovation in Production: Implementing comprehensive training programs in Mobile Journalism (MoJo), empowering editorial teams to produce high-impact, digital-first content.
  • Emerging Technologies: Pioneering the integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to enhance news quality and digital verification, certified by the ASBU and EBU Academy.
2010 – 2023

Regulatory Coordination & Senior Editorial Management

As a Senior News Editor & Media Regulatory Coordinator, Meher spent over a decade ensuring that high-level broadcasts met the most stringent ethical and legal standards.

  • Compliance & Governance: Acting as the primary liaison for national regulatory bodies (HAICA, ISIE), he authored exhaustive compliance reports and enforced rigorous standards during critical periods such as National Elections.
  • Global Event Orchestration: Directed the live coverage and editorial execution for high-profile international events, most notably the 2019 Arab Summit.
  • Strategic Oversight: Maintained total editorial control and quality assurance across complex broadcasting environments.
2006 – 2010

Foundations in Journalism & Field Reporting

Meher’s career is rooted in the core disciplines of newsgathering and dissemination. As a Journalist, Reporter & News Anchor, he developed the foundational skills that inform his current strategic roles:

  • Live Broadcasting: Gained extensive experience as a news anchor, managing real-time information flow during live flashes and broadcasts.
  • In-depth Reporting: Conducted field investigations and interviews focused on socio-economic and political affairs, refining his scriptwriting and analytical capabilities.
